COLD COFFEE

This Chocolate Coffee Breakfast Smoothie is a dessert-worthy treat and a sneaky way to add an extra dose of vegetables to your day

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 frozen banana ($0.10)
  • 1 Tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder ($0.04)
  • 1 Tbsp instant coffee ($0.07)
  • 1 cup milk* ($0.41)
  • 1/2 tsp sugar** ($0.01)

STEPS

  1. Add all of the ingredients to a blender and blend until smooth. Taste and adjust the sweetness to your liking. Serve immediately.
